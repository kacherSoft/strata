## 2. Target Audience

### Primary: AI Power Users & Prompt Engineers

**Profile:**
- Age: 25-45
- Developers, writers, researchers, consultants
- Uses multiple AI tools daily
- Values control, efficiency, customization

**Pain Points:**
- Context switching between apps disrupts flow
- Subscription fatigue (too many AI services)
- Privacy concerns with cloud-based tools

**Key Message:** "Your prompt engineering workflow, supercharged"

### Secondary: Freelancers & Consultants

**Profile:**
- Age: 28-50
- Juggles multiple clients/projects
- Works across different company tools

**Pain Points:**
- Keeping track of tasks across clients
- Maintaining professional communication
- Time management across projects

**Key Message:** "Professional communication in every app"

### Tertiary: Privacy-Conscious Professionals

**Profile:**
- Age: 30-55
- Works with sensitive data (legal, medical, finance)
- Willing to pay premium for privacy

**Pain Points:**
- Cloud tools violate company policies
- Can't use Grammarly/Notion AI at work
- Compliance requirements

**Key Message:** "AI assistance without the privacy nightmare"

## 3. Value Proposition

### The One-Liner
> "Strata is Grammarly meets BYOK meets system-wide access — a Mac AI enhancement tool that works in any app, with your own API keys, keeping your data completely private."

### Problem Statement
Your tasks live in Jira. And Monday. And Asana. And your email. And a Notes file somewhere. You work across 5+ different company systems, but you need YOUR system.

### Solution Statement
Strata is your personal layer above the corporate tools. One view across ALL your projects, with AI superpowers that work everywhere on your Mac.

### Key Differentiators

| Differentiator | Description | Why It Matters |
|----------------|-------------|----------------|
| **System-Wide Access** | Works in ANY app on your Mac | No copy-paste, no context switching |
| **BYOK** | Bring Your Own Key (Gemini, z.ai, custom) | Cost control, privacy, flexibility |
| **100% Local** | All data stays on your Mac | No cloud, no tracking, no account |
| **Custom AI Modes** | Create your own enhancement styles | Personalized to your workflow |

SAN FRANCISCO, CA — February 25, 2026 — KacherSoft today announced the launch of Strata, a native macOS task manager that brings AI enhancement to any application on your Mac. Designed for freelancers, consultants, and professionals juggling multiple projects, Strata combines powerful task management with system-wide AI capabilities—all while keeping data 100% local.

Strata's flagship feature, Inline Enhance, allows users to select text in any application and enhance it with AI using a simple keyboard shortcut (Cmd+Opt+E). Unlike cloud-based writing assistants, Strata uses the user's own API key from providers like Gemini or z.ai, ensuring complete privacy and cost control.

• Native Mac Design: SwiftUI with liquid glass dark mode effects
• 100% Local Storage: All data stays on your Mac

For more information, visit kachersoft.com/strata
