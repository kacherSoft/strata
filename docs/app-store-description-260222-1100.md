**Strata - Your AI, Anywhere on Your Mac**

Press ⌘⌥E. Enhance text. Done.

**Strata** brings AI enhancement to EVERY app on your Mac. Mail, Notes, Slack, Safari, VS Code—if you can select text, you can enhance it. Like Grammarly, but with YOUR AI models and complete privacy.

---

**THE KILLER FEATURE: INLINE ENHANCE**

Stop copy-pasting to ChatGPT. With Strata:

1. Select text in ANY app
2. Press ⌘⌥E
3. Choose your enhancement mode
4. Text transforms in-place

Works in Mail, Notes, Slack, Safari, Figma, and 100+ more apps. No copy-paste. No context switching.

---

**BYOK (Bring Your Own Key)**

Your API key. Your costs. Your privacy.

• Use Gemini, z.ai, or any compatible provider
• Keys stored securely in macOS Keychain
• Nothing sent to us—ever
• Complete cost control

Built-in modes:

• **Enhance Prompt** - Transform ideas into detailed prompts

Plus custom AI modes for your unique workflow.
