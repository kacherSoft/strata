## 2. Feature Descriptions

### Inline Enhance (System-Wide)

**Headline:**
> One Shortcut. Any App. Instant Enhancement.

**Body:**
Press Cmd+Opt+E anywhere on your Mac. Select text in Mail, Notes, Safari, Slack, VS Code - any app. Strata captures it, enhances it with AI, and replaces it in place. No copy. No paste. No context switching.

The enhancement appears right where you're working. Like magic, but it's just smart engineering.

**Use Cases:**
- Fix grammar in an email draft
- Polish a Slack message before sending
- Improve clarity in a Google Doc
- Refine a prompt in your AI tool
- Edit code comments in your IDE

**Technical Details:**
Works in native Mac apps, Electron apps (VS Code, Slack, Discord), and all major browsers (Chrome, Safari, Arc, Firefox).

---

### BYOK (Bring Your Own Key)

**Headline:**
> Your Keys. Your Models. Your Control.

**Body:**
Why pay for another AI subscription when you already have API access? Strata connects to YOUR accounts. Gemini. z.ai. Custom providers. Whatever you use.

This means:
- You control costs (pay only what you use)
- You choose models (flash for speed, pro for quality)
- You own your data (nothing routes through us)
- No subscription fatigue (one less monthly bill)

**Setup:**
Add your API key in Settings. Stored securely in macOS Keychain. Never leaves your device. Done.

## 9. Voice & Tone Notes

**Brand Voice:**
- Confident but not arrogant
- Technical but accessible
- Direct without being cold
- Human, not corporate

**What We Say:**
- "Works in ANY app"
- "Your keys, your costs"
- "100% local, 100% private"

**What We Don't Say:**
- "Revolutionary AI technology"
- "Best-in-class enhancement"
- "Industry-leading privacy"
- Corporate jargon

**Writing Rules:**
- Lead with benefits, support with features
- Specific numbers over vague claims
- Short sentences for punch
- Long sentences for rhythm
- No exclamation points in headlines
- Maximum one emoji per social post
